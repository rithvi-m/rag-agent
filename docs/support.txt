How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email and check your inbox for a reset link. The link expires in 30 minutes.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Log in to your account, go to Settings then Billing, and click Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Why is my payment failing?
Common reasons include expired card, insufficient funds, or billing address mismatch. Update your payment method in Settings then Billing.

How do I contact support?
Email us at support@example.com or use the live chat in the bottom right corner. We respond within 24 hours.
